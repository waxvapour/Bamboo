exit

Exit from a program, shell or log out of a Unix network.

Syntax
      exit

If supported will exit you from the program, shell or log you out of network.

Alternatives: logout, lo, bye, quit, also Ctrl-D.


"Either I'm dead or my watch has stopped" ~ Groucho Marx's last words

Related linux commands:

logout - Exit a login shell
Equivalent Windows commands: EXIT
