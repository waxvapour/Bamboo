DEFRAG

Defragment hard drive. 

Syntax
      DEFRAG <volume> [-a] [-f] [-v] [-?]

Options 
  volume  drive letter or mount point (d: or d:\vol\mountpoint)
  -a      Analyze only
  -f      Force defragmentation even if free space is low
  -v      Verbose output

Example:

DEFRAG c:
